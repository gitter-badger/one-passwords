--- layout: null section-type: about title: About --- ## About One Passwords makes your password management easier and safe at the same time. Select one secret passphrase and then use it in combination with the service name that you want to get your password of. When you decide to change your passwords in order to be more safe, simply choose a new secret passphrase. One Passwords doesn't store your passwords anywhere, because the generated passwords are hashes of Bitcoin's Blockchain. You can find the source code of the web app here
